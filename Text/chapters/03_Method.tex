\textcite{Liu.2012} summarizes the most influential papers in the area of sentiment analysis or opinion mining, a procedure to determine whether a snippet of text, be it in the form of a commentary on a product, post on social media or article in a newspaper, communicates a positive or negative message about the topic at stake using natural language processing (NLP). Application possibilities of these tools are ample, ranging from businesses improving their products through online reviews to automated fraud and insider trading detection through monitoring analysts messaging behaviour and are thus of major importance for businesses, individuals and policy makers alike.
